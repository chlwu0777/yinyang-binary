## 一个被遗忘的真相 / A Forgotten Truth

在文字诞生之前，在数学被命名之前，在这个星球上第一台计算机被通电之前——有人用一根线的"断开"与"连续"，建立了一套理解世界的编码系统。

Before writing existed, before mathematics had a name, before the first computer on this planet was ever powered on — someone built an encoding system to understand the world, using nothing but the "break" and "continuity" of a single line.

那个人，传说叫**伏羲**。

That person, legend says, was **Fu Xi**.

他画的完整横线「——」代表阳，断开的横线「- -」代表阴。三根线的组合产生了**八卦**，覆盖八种基本状态。六根线的组合产生了**六十四卦**，穷尽了六十四种可能。

His solid line "——" represents Yang; the broken line "- -" represents Yin. Three lines combine into the **Eight Trigrams** (8 states). Six lines combine into the **64 Hexagrams** (64 states).

大多数人把八卦当作占卜的工具。很少有人注意到一个惊人的数学事实：

Most people see the trigrams as fortune-telling tools. Few notice an astonishing mathematical fact:

> **阳爻 = 1，阴爻 = 0。八卦 = 3-bit 编码（2³ = 8），六十四卦 = 完整的 6-bit 状态空间（2⁶ = 64）。**
>
> **Yang line = 1, Yin line = 0. Eight Trigrams = 3-bit encoding (2³ = 8). 64 Hexagrams = complete 6-bit state space (2⁶ = 64).**

这不是牵强附会，这是精确的数学同构。

This is not a loose metaphor — it is exact mathematical isomorphism.

## 一封跨越万里的信 / A Letter Across Ten Thousand Miles

1703 年，德国数学家**莱布尼茨**收到法国传教士白晋从北京寄来的**邵雍先天六十四卦方位图**。他展开这张图，震惊地发现：六十四个由横线组成的符号，正是从 `000000` 到 `111111` 的完整二进制序列。

In 1703, German mathematician **Leibniz** received **Shao Yong's Earlier Heaven 64 Hexagram diagram** from French missionary Bouvet in Beijing. Unfolding it, he was stunned: the 64 symbols of horizontal lines formed the complete binary sequence from `000000` to `111111`.

他在论文中写道——

He wrote in his paper:

> *"这证明了二进制算术的深远意义，因为中国古人在数千年前就已经抵达了同样的体系。"*
>
> *"This proves the profound significance of binary arithmetic, for the ancient Chinese arrived at the same system thousands of years ago."*

这是人类思想史上最动人的跨文明时刻之一。东方与西方，相隔万里和数千年，独立抵达了同一个真理。

One of the most stirring cross-civilization moments in human intellectual history. East and West, separated by ten thousand miles and thousands of years, independently arrived at the same truth.

## 一条从未断开的线 / An Unbroken Line

从伏羲的阴阳爻，到文王的六十四卦，到邵雍的加一倍法，到莱布尼茨的二进制算术，到布尔的逻辑代数，到香农的信息论，到冯·诺依曼的计算机架构，到你此刻正在阅读这段文字的这块屏幕——

From Fu Xi's Yin-Yang lines, to King Wen's 64 hexagrams, to Shao Yong's doubling method, to Leibniz's binary arithmetic, to Boole's logic algebra, to Shannon's information theory, to von Neumann's computer architecture, to the screen on which you are reading these very words —

**这是一条横跨五千年、连接东方与西方的思想之线。它从未断开。**

**This is a thread of thought spanning 5,000 years, connecting East and West. It has never been broken.**

「二进制之道」把这条线变成了你可以亲身经历的旅程。

*The Tao of Binary* turns this thread into a journey you can experience firsthand.

## 功能一览 / Features

### ☯ 太极交互 / Taiji Interaction
点击太极图，亲眼见证"一分为二"的离散化过程——从混沌的太极，到阴阳两仪，到四象，到八卦，到六十四卦。

Click the Taiji symbol and witness the discretization process — from undifferentiated Taiji to Yin-Yang, to Four Images, to Eight Trigrams, to 64 Hexagrams.

### 📜 先贤长廊 / Hall of Masters
八位改变人类认知方式的先贤：伏羲、周文王、孔子、邵雍、莱布尼茨、高岛吞象、卫礼贤、荣格。五千年的知识接力，每一棒都有故事。

Eight masters who changed how humanity thinks: Fu Xi, King Wen, Confucius, Shao Yong, Leibniz, Takashima, Wilhelm, Jung. A 5,000-year relay of knowledge.

### 🔢 六十四卦浏览器 / 64 Hexagram Browser
8×8 交互式网格，每一卦逐爻解读，同时显示对应的二进制数值。鼠标悬停即可查看爻辞释义。支持中/英/双语切换。

Interactive 8×8 grid. Each hexagram with line-by-line commentary alongside its binary value. Hover over any line to see its statement. Supports CN/EN/bilingual toggle.

### 🎮 三种游戏模式 / Three Game Modes

| 模式 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| **沙盒 Sandbox** | 自由翻转阴阳爻，探索 64 种状态 / Freely flip Yin-Yang lines, explore all 64 states |
| **解谜 Puzzle** | 用二进制思维完成状态迁移挑战 / Complete state-transition challenges with binary thinking |
| **探索 Explorer** | 可视化 64 态空间，发现相邻卦象的数学关系 / Visualize the 64-state space, discover adjacency |

### 🕰️ 滚动叙事时间轴 / Scroll Narrative Timeline
从太极到计算机，8 个历史里程碑随滚动浮现。五千年历程，一屏到底。

From Taiji to Computer, 8 historical milestones revealed as you scroll. Five millennia, one continuous scroll.
